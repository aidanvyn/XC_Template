56th Holly-Duane Raffin Festival of Races Mens 5000 Meters D1 JV Boys

Sat Sep 14 2024   Meet results

"The Ann Arbor Skyline JV Boys team delivered a strong performance at the 56th Holly-Duane Raffin Festival of Races, finishing in 3rd place overall with 72 points. The team’s impressive consistency and depth were on full display during the 5,000-meter race.Freshman Oskar MacArthur led the Skyline pack, finishing in 17:57.7, followed closely by freshman Martin Gehrke at 17:58.8. Both runners broke the 18-minute barrier, setting the pace for the team.Sophomore Dylan Hanley clocked in at 18:13.9, with junior Parker Razelun finishing in 18:21.8. Rounding out the top five for Skyline, sophomore Santiago Valenzuela crossed the line at 18:56.7, capping off a solid team performance.The close grouping of Skyline’s top runners helped secure their strong placement, setting the stage for a promising season ahead."
