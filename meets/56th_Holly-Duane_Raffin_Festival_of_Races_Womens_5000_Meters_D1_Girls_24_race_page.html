56th Holly-Duane Raffin Festival of Races Womens 5000 Meters D1 Girls

Sat Sep 14 2024   Meet results

"Ann Arbor Skyline's Varsity girls team gave it their all at the 56th Holly-Duane Raffin Festival of Races, finishing 8th overall with 204 points. The team faced tough competition but held their own against some of the best in the state.Leading the way for Skyline was sophomore Irie Scrase, who put up a stellar performance, crossing the line with a personal record of 19:48.9. Freshman Elin Tenbrink followed, clocking in at 21:47.4, showing great promise for the future of Skyline’s team.Other notable finishes included sophomore Isla Tharp, who crossed the line at 21:52.3, and sophomore Alison Kauffman at 22:09.9. Rounding out the top five was sophomore Mara Mocanu with a time of 22:13.0.Skyline’s runners demonstrated their resilience and determination as they competed against a tough field, proving once again that they have the potential to rise through the ranks as the season progresses."
